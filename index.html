Royal Hobart International Wine Show

First held in 1975 it has grown steadily in popularity and status to become the second largest wine show in the country. Its reputation as the showcase of a wide range of high quality wines of all styles has blossomed as part of this development, with an ever-increasing stream of high praise coming from judges, wine writers, exhibitors from all over Australia and the general public.
